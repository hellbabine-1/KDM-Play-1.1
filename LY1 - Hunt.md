# Babel Hunting Logs  
## LY1

We got Ascalon, Scout, Zack and Erza going out. 

Ascalon has all the armor and the bone dart and founding stone

Scout has the bone dagger, cloth and founding stone

Erza and Zach have the founding stone and cloth

The survivors encounter some Dead Weed (18). They manage to get some, but no one is hurt to make use of it before it crumble to dust 

Next, the survivors encounter some Soggy Prints from their quary. They manage to retrive some hide from the footprints. 

While distracted, they hear a huge metallic crash in the distance. They decide to follow it and found some corpse to harvest. They get a bone, hide and organ, before continuing on their hunt (event 100)

A short while after, Zach is Overloaded with emotion (event 13). He start making some random noise with the clanking of his gear (got Corpolalia)

The survivors find their quarry.
